## 1.0.0 — 2026-05-27

- **Initial Stable Release**:
  - Unified, cross-platform Augmented Reality (AR) library wrapping ARKit (iOS) and ARCore (Android) behind a clean, pure-Dart interface.
  - Interactive distance measurement (tapping points in 3D space) and volumetric room scanner (X, Y, Z coordinates).
  - Acoustic chamber volume calculations ($m^3$) and export capabilities to acoustic simulation systems like `neom_levitation`.
  - Offline stubs for testing on desktop or non-AR hardware.
  - Fully translated in English, Spanish, French, and German.
